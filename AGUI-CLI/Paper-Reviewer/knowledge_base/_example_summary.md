**Citation (MLA 8):** Korinek, Anton. "AI Agents for Economic Research." *National Bureau of Economic Research*, Working Paper No. 34202, 2025. https://www.nber.org/papers/w34202.

## Content Summary

This paper demystifies AI agents - autonomous LLM-based systems that plan, use tools, and execute multi-step research tasks - and provides hands-on guidance for economists to build their own. Korinek documents that agent task competence has been doubling roughly every seven months since 2019, with current agents handling tasks of approximately 50 minutes autonomously. The paper argues agents are transitioning from text-completion tools to research assistants capable of literature review, data analysis, and drafting, while cautioning that hallucination risk and the need for human oversight remain significant constraints.
